# ChefStash Marketing Strategy

**Document:** MARKETING.md  
**Product:** ChefStash (Recipe Vault)

## 1. Viability Classification
**Tier 4: Niche**  
Hard to explain the technical "extraction" value prop in a fast-paced ad.

## 2. Target Audience & Ad Platforms
- **Primary Platform:** Pinterest (Food/Recipe boards) and organic SEO.
- **Targeting:** Home cooks, meal preppers.

## 3. Core Marketing Hook (Ad Copy)
"Skip the 5,000-word life story and the auto-playing ads. Paste any food blog URL and ChefStash instantly extracts just the recipe. Keep your screen awake while cooking."

## 4. Geographic & Pricing Strategy
- **Tier 1:** US/UK primarily. $19.99 one-time purchase.
